The Queen

Her Majesty The Queen

Her Majesty Queen Yeji was born on March 2, 2000, in Geum Imperial House in Busan. She is the daughter of the then Imperial Geum Prince, who later became King Baek II, and the Imperial Geum Princess (later Queen Yu'er, now Queen Yu'er the Queen Mother). The princess was born second in line after her father. On April 20, 2000, King Se Hee passed away, and King Baek II ascended to the throne, making Princess Yeji the First Imperial Geum Princess in history.
