About His royal Highness The Duke Of Borang

His Royal Highness The Prince Tae Oh Duke Of Borang started the royal life in 2019 after his marriage to Her Majesty The Queen, The Duke is The Serene captain of the Korean Marine And Airforce as Duke of Borang the Duke made serious measures to ensure equality and greater conditions of life in Borang in the North region of the Kingdom of Korea, The prince is also Patron of more than 234 Social charities

The Prince Tae Oh

Born into a common family in the Kingdom Of Korea, His royal Highness was in touch with the regular population and was able to get really close to the general public, as a prince he always state the Queen is my first priority our people is also a priority
